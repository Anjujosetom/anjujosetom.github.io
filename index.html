About Me

I am a Data Science Leader and Principal Data Modeller at Queensland Treasury, driving data-informed policy, financial governance, and compliance automation. My expertise bridges data science, machine learning, predictive analytics, and government analytics, with proven experience in Databricks, Power BI, SQL, SAP HANA, and Azure.

With a PhD in Computer Vision (awarded Best PhD Thesis in Kerala and ranked 4th best in India, 2020), I transitioned from an accomplished academic career to become a trusted technical leader in government analytics. My journey spans postdoctoral research at INRIA (French National Institute for Research in Digital Science and Technology), AI-driven defence projects at QUT with the Dept of Defence, and leadership in machine learning–powered compliance automation at QRO.

I have also been recognised by the Australian Government through the prestigious Global Talent Visa program, awarded for an internationally distinguished record of exceptional professional achievements and contributions to the data science community.

Expertise

  • Machine Learning & AI – Predictive analytics, anomaly detection, NLP-driven document intelligence.

Projects & Impact

1. Dept of Defense -SME Revolution Aerospace – QUT Collaboartion Project, Australia

Low-Cost Cognitive Electronic Warfare (C-EW) System – A $1.5M Defence project focused on advanced AI systems for electronic warfare. [Project Link]

I led project management from initiation, tracking research objectives, coordinating with chief investigators, and mentoring the team. I managed executive reporting, stakeholder engagement, progress tracking, and milestone delivery. I also represented the team at major international events, including the Eurosatory Conference in Paris.

2. Data Repurposing (DARE) Project – French National Institute for Research in Computer Science and Automation (INRIA), France

Data Repurposing (DARE) Project – A two-year research initiative developing new compression paradigms for large-scale image and video databases. [Project Report] | [DARE Team Members]

As a Postdoctoral Fellow and Project Manager, I coordinated research delivery, liaised with multiple stakeholders, and ensured strategic alignment. This role gave me deep experience in international project management, cross-cultural collaboration, and delivery of high-impact outcomes.

3. Machine Learning Compliance Model – Queensland Treasury

Designed and implemented the first ML-driven compliance model for the a well known Home Exemption scheme. The model achieved 98% accuracy in predicting and validating exemption claims. This initiative was highly appreciated by leadership and demonstrated the power of applied ML in compliance analytics.

My role included building the proof-of-concept, collaborating with stakeholders to gather requirements, and presenting results to leadership. I also developed Natural Language Processing (NLP) pipelines to process data from over 4000 confidential SharePoint case folders, extracting insights from more than 29,000 official PDF and Word files.
